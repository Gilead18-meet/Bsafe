Who are we?

We are B-Safe, the problem of babies being forgotten in cars has been a growing problem. In the last 10 years there have been over 650 cases of babies being forggoten in cars in Ireal alone.

Our product is a combination of a device that has a weight-scale and an app that are connected together via bluetooth. If the person driving leaves the vehicle while there is still weight on the booster, a message pops up on their phone. We are business to buisness and sell our device to saftey-seat companies. This way our product is an extra layer of security that comes with an already essential product
